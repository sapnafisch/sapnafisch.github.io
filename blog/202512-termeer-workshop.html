Termeer Scholars Programming
Grounding

I had the privilege of joining the inaugural Termeer Scholars 2025 programming in Boston — two days of immersive workshops, panel discussions, and conversations designed to help academic researchers think seriously about translating their work toward patient impact. The programming brought together nine scholars from research institutions across the country, alongside an impressive range of voices from pharma, venture capital, academia, tech transfer, and small and midsize biotech. The sessions covered the translational pathway, values-driven leadership, and what it actually takes to move discoveries from bench to bedside with purpose and clarity. During the programming with 2025 Termeer Scholars, I was reminded that it’s not just the learning or the networking that makes these experiences meaningful, but it’s being surrounded by people who are grounded, open, and genuinely invested in each other’s growth. Grateful to be part of this community and looking forward to engaging more in future.
